About us

Our mission is to conserve nature and reduce the most pressing threats to the diversity of life on Earth.

  • Advocates for sustainable land management, fostering awareness and action towards achieving SDG goal Life on Land

  • Educate, inspire, and collaborate to conserve and restore terrestrial ecosystems for a thriving planet.

  • Support our mission through donations, volunteering, spreading awareness, and adopting sustainable practices in daily life.

  • Globally, focusing on areas vulnerable to land degradation, deforestation, and biodiversity loss.
