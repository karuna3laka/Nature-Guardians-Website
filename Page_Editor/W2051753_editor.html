-What I have Done In This Project-

Task 1

In my web project, I implemented a progressive profile form using prompts instead of traditional forms. The system guides users through the process step by step, presenting concise and clear prompts for information such as their name or username. These prompts are organized into categories, making it easy for users to understand and complete the form. For example, the prompts cover various aspects like personal details, tasks, qualifications, and others, depending on the theme of the website.
By breaking down the information gathering process into manageable steps and categories, users can easily navigate and provide the necessary information. By incorporating this approach, users are less overwhelmed and more likely to complete the profile accurately.
This progressive profile form consists of at least 12 prompts divided into 3 steps, ensuring a comprehensive user profile is built while maintaining simplicity and clarity.

Task 2

Upon hovering over a thumbnail, users experience a visual change, such as altering the opacity of the image, indicating interactivity.
